ID  NAME        DESCRIPTION
=== ==========  ================================
1   Cleric      Wise and charismatic. Typically a healer who wields divine power in the service of their diety.
2   Fighter     Strong and tough or fast and tough. Master of combat and knowledgable in weapons and armor.
3   Rogue       Fast and intelligent. Highly skilled and commonly duplicitous. Commonly a seeker of treasures.
4   Wizard      Intelligent and wise. Weaves magic spells that manipulate reality using long studied arcane knowledge.
